Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

MundhwaTraffic.com (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides real-time traffic visibility for Mundhwa Bridge in Pune. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

  • Basic device data: browser type, operating system, screen size, and IP region (city-level only).
  • Analytics data: anonymous usage statistics such as page views, watch time, and interactions.
  • Ad-related data: non-identifiable information used to display ads, prevent fraud, and improve ad performance.
  • Push notification data: if you subscribe to alerts, we store a non-identifiable subscription token.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No personal identity information (name, email, phone number, address).
  • No precise GPS or live location.
  • No camera access, microphone access, or device files.

How We Use Your Data

  • To improve traffic accuracy and user experience.
  • To send browser-based traffic notifications if you opt in.
  • To measure website performance and stability.
  • To serve advertising and ensure compliance with ad network requirements.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate and improve our website:

  • Ezoic – advertising optimization, ad delivery, and analytics.
    Ezoic’s privacy policy: https://g.ezoic.net/privacy/mundhwatraffic.com
  • Google Analytics – anonymous traffic and usage measurement.
  • Google AdSense / Google Ad Manager – ad serving and reporting.
  • YouTube – embedded livestream video player.
  • OneSignal – optional push notifications.

These services may use cookies, local storage, or similar technologies to function properly, measure performance, or deliver ads. You may disable cookies in your browser at any time.

Data Protection & Security

We do not sell or share personally identifiable information with any third party. We use encryption (HTTPS) and security best practices to protect your data and minimize misuse, fraud, or unauthorized access.

Your Choices

  • You may disable cookies in your browser settings.
  • You may unsubscribe from push notifications at any time.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising via Google Ads Settings or industry opt-out tools.
