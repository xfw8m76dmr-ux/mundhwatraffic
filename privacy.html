Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

MundhwaTraffic.com (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides real-time traffic visibility for Mundhwa Bridge in Pune. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

  • Basic device data: browser type, operating system, screen size, and IP region (city-level only).
  • Analytics data: anonymous usage statistics such as page views, watch time, and interactions.
  • Push notification data: if you subscribe to alerts, we store a non-identifiable subscription token.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No personal identity information (name, email, phone number, address).
  • No precise GPS or live location.
  • No camera access, microphone access, or device files.

How We Use Your Data

  • To improve traffic accuracy and user experience.
  • To send browser-based traffic notifications if you opt in.
  • To measure website performance and stability.
  • To serve Google Ads based on non-personalized ad signals.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

  • Google Analytics – anonymous traffic measurement.
  • Google AdSense / Google Ad Manager – ad delivery.
  • YouTube – embedded livestream player.
  • OneSignal – optional push notifications.

These services may use cookies or local storage to function properly. You may disable cookies in your browser at any time.

Data Protection & Security

We do not sell or share user data with any third party except the services listed above. We use encryption (HTTPS) and security best practices to protect your information.

Your Choices

  • You may disable cookies in your browser.
  • You may unsubscribe from push notifications at any time.
  • You may opt out of personalized ads using Google Ads Settings.
