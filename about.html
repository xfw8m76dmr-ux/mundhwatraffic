About MundhwaTraffic.com

MundhwaTraffic.com is a community-driven real-time traffic platform built to help Pune commuters check Mundhwa Bridge congestion instantly. The project is powered by RoadX smart camera technology, automated congestion detection, and live data streaming.

Our goal is simple: help people avoid unnecessary traffic and travel smarter. Instead of relying on delayed navigation apps, users can see an actual live view of the road before starting their trip.

Thousands of commuters, riders, and office-goers check this camera daily. We continue to upgrade our systems with better AI, faster alerts, and more accurate congestion insights.
